Angular 8 Lifecycle

ngOnChanges - Called After a bound input change
ngOnInit - Called once the component is initialized
ngDoCheck - Called during every change detection run
ngAfterContentInit - Called after content (ng-content) has been project into view
ngAfterContentChecked - Called every time the projected content has been checked
ngAfterViewInit - Called after the components's view (and child views) has been initialized
ngAfterViewChecked - Called every time the view (and child views) have been checked
ngOnDestroy - Called once the component is about to be destroyed
